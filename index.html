A Curiouser kind of honey Story

Like the Mad Hatter himself, Emily & Cody Hill march to the beat of their own drum — or rather, the hum of their own hive. What started as a curiosity about bees became a full-blown love affair with the art of beekeeping in the rolling Alabama countryside.

Every jar of Mad Nectar honey is raw, unfiltered, and alive with the flavors of the local wildflowers, clover fields, and Southern landscapes that the bees call home. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just pure, honest honey.

"We didn't fall into beekeeping — we tumbled in, headfirst, just like Alice."
